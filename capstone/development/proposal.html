Capstone Project: Proposal

What is your topic and who is your audience?

I plan to do a data visualization or narrative project about time. One idea I currently have in mind is about the average person's progress throughout each year of their life. It will be a timeline of sorts, but users will be able to explore what each age is expected to have done on average. For example, by age 5 most people are expected to have started exhibiting increased independence and cognitive thinking. This project could also go into how these expectations are changing with the rise of AI.

Identify lenses we have discussed in class that your topic may rely on most.

I think I'll be focusing on data visualization, but this project also deals with speculative design and dystopian narratives when thinking about things from an AI perspective. For example, because of the rise of AI many children have started exhibiting a lack of critical thinking skills in their development. This is due to relying on shortcuts like AI chatbots for schoolwork and such. It also goes deeper, as AI and tech has touched upon human relationships and the way we interact with each other as well. A lot of speculation has been done on the negative effects of new technology on the way we think and live, which is something that could be explored in this project.

What are some of the features you imagine your project/product would include?

I've been thinking about either going towards a more neutral data visualization project where I simply display the data and text or a more storytelling-based project where the user could be immersed in a role. The data visualization one would be more simple in its execution and focus on the numbers and research to do most of the work. The storytelling one would be more complicated and need a lot more thinking through but I feel like it would be more fun. Both would end up using GSAP's Split Text library heavily, since this project relies a lot on text. I haven't decided exactly how I want to do this project, but both ways have their own pros and cons that I'm considering.

Are there possible tangents, challenges, concerns, or vague ideas related to your ideas?

The biggest challenge right now would just be fully outlining my project. I only have a vague concept of how I want everything to look like, so I need to figure out exactly what the final project would be. Overall, I just need more time and thought to develop this idea to see how everything works out.

Who is your target audience?

My target audience would probably be people in the same generation or age range as me. So people from high school to people in their early-to-mid 20s. I feel like that's the demographic of people who are most impacted and concerned with the future of AI and how it impacts our future right now. What I had in mind for this project was just to get people thinking about how we as humans are changing rapidly in this new age of technology, and whether the consequences of these changes outweigh the benefits. I also think that since we're the first generation to really live through the AI boom and witness its effects firsthand on academia and the people around us, we need to really think about what we ultimately want for ourselves.
